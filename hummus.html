Description

Hummus is the perfect side dish for all meals. It is great for get-togethers, picnics and just ideal for a snack.

Aside from being vegan, it is a healthy dish that can be accompanied by carrots and celery, pita chips or bread.

I also really like adding some toppings (paprika, olives - really recommend) to my hummus but it tastes delicious without them as well.

Recipe

- 1 can of chickpeas (keep the liquid, you'll need it)

- 1/2 clove of garlic

- 1/2 lemon

- Dash of olive oil

- Paprika

  1. Chop garlic clove into small pieces
  2. Place chickpeas (drain them first, but save the liquid) in the blender and blend until smooth
  3. Add garlic, lemon and olive oil
  4. Add the liquid from the can (not all, add until you get a smooth mix, not a liquid one)
  5. Taste and add more ingredients if necessary
  6. Serve with toppings of your choosing! (Olives, paprika, etc.) also, eat with pita bread!
