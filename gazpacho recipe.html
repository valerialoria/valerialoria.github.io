Recipe

- 3 tomatoes (no peel)

- 1 cucumber (no peel)

- 1 teaspoon paprika

- 1 clove of garlic (better if chopped)

- 1/3 cup of red wine

- 1/4 cup of wine vinegar/apple cider vinegar

- 1/2 cup of olive oil

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 1 teaspoon of sugar

- 1 lemon

- Toppings: purple onion, cucumber, bell peppers (any or all colors), and hard boiled egg... or choose your own

  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix until theyâ€™ve become liquid and smooth
