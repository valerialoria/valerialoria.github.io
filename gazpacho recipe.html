Description

Gazpacho is a traditional Spanish cold soup made from tomatoes and some other things. It is a perfect dish for summer.

Some people even make gazpacho out of watermelon, cucumber and other veggies and fruits. Feel free to experiment with these.

My favorite gazpacho will always be the traditional tomato-base but I will keep trying to make some variations of it!

Recipe

- 3 tomatoes (no peel)

- 1 cucumber (no peel)

- 1 teaspoon paprika

- 1 clove of garlic (better if chopped)

- 1/3 cup of red wine

- 1/4 cup of wine vinegar/apple cider vinegar

- 1/2 cup of olive oil

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 1 teaspoon of sugar

- 1 lemon

- Toppings: purple onion, cucumber, bell peppers (any or all colors), and hard boiled egg... or choose your own

  1. Blend together tomatoes, then add the cucumber
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix until theyâ€™ve become liquid and smooth
  3. Place in fridge for about 30 min
  4. Serve cold and with toppings of choosing on top
